Traitor Roles

The traitor team is one of the two primary teams in play in a game of Trouble in Terrorist Town.

The main goal of the traitor team is to eliminate all members of the innocent team, including any detectives. In some cases, they also need to take out monsters, jesters, or independents that could prevent their victory.

Members of the traitor team have their roles known to each other, and jesters are also known to traitors. Additionally, traitors have the ability to see players who are dead but have not had their bodies found as "missing in action" on the scoreboard. This gives the traitor team a significant advantage over the innocent team, whose roles are not known to each other, and who cannot see MIA players on the scoreboard. To address this, most games have a higher number of innocent players than traitors.

Traitors often have access to weapons or abilities that give them additional advantages against the innocent team, but can give away their allegiance if not used in secret.
