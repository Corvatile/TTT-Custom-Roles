Innocent Roles

The innocent team is one of the two primary teams in play in a game of Trouble in Terrorist Town.

The main goal of the innocent team is to identify and eliminate all members of the traitor team. In some cases, they also need to take out monsters, jesters, or independents that could prevent their victory.

Members of the innocent team do not have their roles known to any other players, and they usually lack a definitive method to prove their innocence. This puts the innocent team at a significant disadvantage compared to the traitor team, whose roles are known to each other. To address this, most games have a higher number of innocent players than traitors.

Detectives are also part of the innocent team, sharing the same objective, but their allegiance is known to all players.

The Innocent is the base member of the innocent team and has no special abilities.
