Detective Roles

Detectives are part of the innocent team, however they have unique advantages and disadvantages compared to other innocents.

The main goal of detectives, and by extension the innocent team, is to identify and eliminate all members of the traitor team. In some cases, they also need to take out monsters, jesters, or independents that could prevent their victory.

Detectives have their role known to all players, as such they usually act as the de facto leader of the innocent team. They also have the ability to search dead bodies of players and uncover their role and information about their death. This information is often vital to the success of the innocent team and can make detectives high priority targets for the traitors.
