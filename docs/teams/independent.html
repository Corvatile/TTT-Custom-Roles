Independent Roles

Independents have unique win conditions for each role. There is very little in common between different independents, and they can play completely different to each other. Typically, there are very few independents in each round.

Most independents do not have their role known to any other players, and are not part of a team. Even if there are multiple different independents in the same round, they will often be competing against each other either directly or indirectly. However, there are exceptions to this as some independents have a goal of converting other players to their team. In these cases those players will be aware of each other's roles and can win together.

Some independents are made aware of jesters, and have the ability to see players who are dead but have not had their bodies found as "missing in action" on the scoreboard. These abilities are usually reserved for independents whose goal requires killing other players. Independents with a more passive goal will tend not to have these abilities.
