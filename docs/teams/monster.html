Monster Roles

The monster team has no members by default, but it can be configured to act as a third team fighting against both the innocent team and the traitor team.

The main goal of the monster team is to eliminate all members of the innocent team and the traitor team. In some cases, they also need to take out jesters or independents that could prevent their victory.

Members of the monster team have their roles known to each other, and jesters are also known to monsters. Additionally, monsters have the ability to see players who are dead but have not had their bodies found as "missing in action" on the scoreboard. This makes the monster team very similar to the traitor team and gives them a significant advantage over the innocent team, whose roles are not known to each other, and who cannot see MIA players on the scoreboard. To address this, most games have a higher number of innocent players than monsters and traitors.

Monsters often have access to weapons or abilities that give them additional advantages against the innocent team and the traitor team, but can give away their allegiance if not used in secret.

The following roles can be configured to be part of the monster team instead of their default team: Hive Mind, Mad Scientist, Parasite, Vampire, and Zombie.
